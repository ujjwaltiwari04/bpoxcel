About BPOXCEL

Building Stronger Workforces,
One Placement at a Time

BPOXCEL is a Gurugram-based recruitment and workforce solutions company with over 6 years of industry experience. Headquartered near Passport Office, Sector-18, Gurugram, we have been a trusted growth partner for businesses across Delhi NCR and throughout India.

We specialize in end-to-end recruitment — from talent identification and screening to final onboarding — ensuring businesses get the right people at the right time. Our outsourcing capabilities allow companies to delegate non-core functions with confidence, backed by experienced professionals and streamlined processes.

Beyond traditional staffing, BPOXCEL integrates modern AI agents & chatbots into client workflows, helping organizations automate customer interactions and candidate engagement. Our business-first approach ensures that every solution we deliver is aligned with measurable outcomes for our clients.
